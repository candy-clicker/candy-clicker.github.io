About Candy Clicker

Candy Clicker is an incremental game where you make candies by clicking. Keep clicking to generate more candies and upgrade your candy production equipment for faster results.

Game Features

  • Simple one-click gameplay
  • Multiple upgrades available
  • Achievements to unlock
  • Auto-clicker features

How to Play

  • Click to produce candies
  • Buy upgrades with candies
  • Unlock achievements
  • Compete with friends

Controls

  • Left Click - Make candy
  • Space - Make candy
  • ESC - Pause game
  • F - Fullscreen
