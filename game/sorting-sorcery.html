Sorting Sorcery

Sorting Sorcery is an engaging puzzle game that challenges players to sort magical objects and restore order to the enchanted world. With its blend of logic and magic, Sorting Sorcery offers a captivating experience that will keep players entertained for hours.

Game Description

In Sorting Sorcery, you step into the shoes of a talented wizard tasked with organizing a chaotic collection of enchanted items. The game features two main modes: puzzle mode and time trial mode. Puzzle mode allows players to solve increasingly complex sorting challenges at their own pace, while time trial mode tests their speed and accuracy against the clock.

  • Graphics: The game boasts stunning 2D visuals, bringing the magical world to life with vibrant colors and charming animations.
  • Sound: Immerse yourself in the enchanting soundtrack and sound effects that accompany your sorting journey, adding to the mystical atmosphere of the game.
  • Levels: Dive into a variety of levels, each filled with unique sorting puzzles that will test your wits and magical abilities.

How to Play

  • Start the game: Choose your preferred mode and level to begin your sorting adventure.
  • Sorting magic: Use your mouse to drag and drop magical objects into their correct positions based on the sorting criteria provided.
  • Complete the challenges: Solve each puzzle by sorting all the objects correctly within the given time limit or move allowance.
  • Puzzle mode: Take your time to strategize and solve increasingly complex sorting puzzles at your own pace.
  • Time trial mode: Race against the clock to sort objects as quickly and accurately as possible, earning bonus points for speed and precision.

Sorting Sorcery is a spellbinding adventure that combines brain-teasing puzzles with magical elements, making it perfect for players who enjoy both strategy and fantasy.
